Custom T300 Wheel with Integrated Dash

I built this because looking at a stationary, base-mounted dash just doesn't feel right. When you're driving in any sim racing game, you want the telemetry right there in your hands, not mounted to the desk.

The problem? Wheels with seamlessly integrated screens basically don't exist for the Thrustmaster T300 ecosystem — at least not without spending more than the wheelbase itself is worth.

So, I decided to make my own. My goal is simple: bring that high-end, premium GT3/LMP sim racing feel to budget-friendly Thrustmaster hardware.
