Custom T300 Wheel with Integrated Dash

I built this because looking at a stationary, base-mounted dash just doesn't feel right. It just feels cheap and crappy and  wheels with seamlessly integrated screens basically don't exist for the Thrustmaster T300 ecosystem  at least not without spending more than the wheelbase itself is worth.
So, I decided to make my own (and learn fusion 360 as I go since I've never needed to use it), and to bring that premium feel for a way cheaper price
